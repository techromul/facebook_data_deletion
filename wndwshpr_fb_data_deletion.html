Data Deletion Instructions

WindowShopper App

Last Updated: February 26, 2026

Important: This page explains how to delete your data from WindowShopper, including data obtained through Facebook Login.

Overview

WindowShopper respects your privacy and your right to control your personal data. If you signed in using Facebook and want to delete your data from our app, you have two options:

  • Option 1: Delete your account directly in the app (recommended)
  • Option 2: Request data deletion via email

Option 1: Delete Account in the App

The fastest way to delete your data is through the app itself:

Step 1: Open the App

Launch the WindowShopper app on your device and sign in to your account.

Step 2: Navigate to Settings

Tap on your profile icon → Settings → Account Management

Step 3: Delete Account

Scroll down and tap "Delete Account" → Confirm deletion

⏱️ Processing Time: Your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted immediately.

Option 2: Request Deletion via Email

If you cannot access the app or prefer to request deletion via email:

Step 1: Send Email Request

Send an email to: techromul@gmail.com

Subject: Data Deletion Request - WindowShopper

Include:

  • Your full name
  • Email address used for your account
  • Facebook User ID (if available)
  • Confirmation that you want to delete all your data

Step 2: Verification

We will verify your identity and respond within 48 hours to confirm receipt of your request.

Step 3: Deletion

Your data will be permanently deleted within 30 days of verification.

⏱️ Processing Time: Up to 30 days from verification

What Data Will Be Deleted?

When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently removed:

Personal Information

  • Name, email address, and phone number
  • Profile picture and bio
  • Facebook User ID and associated data
  • Account preferences and settings

Activity Data

  • Shopping lists and saved items
  • Search history and recently viewed products
  • Receipt scans and purchase history
  • Location data and search preferences

Store/Seller Data (if applicable)

  • Store information and business details
  • Product listings and images
  • Sales records and analytics
  • Customer interactions

⚠️ Important: Data deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Make sure you want to proceed before confirming.

What Happens After Deletion?

  • Your account will be immediately deactivated
  • You will be logged out of all devices
  • Your data will be permanently deleted from our servers
  • You will receive a confirmation email once deletion is complete
  • You can create a new account anytime using the same or different email

Data Retention

Please note the following exceptions:

Legal Requirements

We may retain certain data if required by law, such as:

  • Transaction records for tax purposes (as required by law)
  • Data subject to legal holds or investigations
  • Anonymized analytics data (cannot be linked back to you)

Backup Systems

Data in backup systems will be deleted within 90 days as part of our regular backup rotation cycle.

Revoking Facebook Permissions

To completely remove WindowShopper's access to your Facebook data:

  1. Go to Facebook Settings → Apps and Websites
  2. Find "WindowShopper" in the list
  3. Click "Remove" to revoke all permissions

This will prevent WindowShopper from accessing any Facebook data, but it does not delete data already stored in our app. You must also delete your account using one of the methods above.
